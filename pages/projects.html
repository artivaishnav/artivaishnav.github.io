RaspberryPi Echo

The RaspberryPiEcho was a side project of mine that I worked on during my winter break of 2016. Following the Instructable created by Toglefritz I was able to turn my RaspberryPi into a fully functional Amazon Alexa device. In the future I hope to add new skills and abilites to my Alexa, and create new skills for others to use as well.
